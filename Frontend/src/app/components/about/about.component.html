Understand Your Insurance Plan & Access The Healthcare You Deserve

About us

We are a social impact project creating a virtual healthcare consultant that allows users to understand their healthcare insurance plans, find required healthcare in their neighbourhood and get a breakdown of healthcare costs.

Features

The Application has the following features



Understand Your Health Plan

Don’t understand your insurance plan and it’s terminologies? Don’t worry. Our platform got you covered! We make it easier for you to understand all information in your health plan in an easier way

Find your provider

What happens when you want to access services included in your health plan? We help you understand costs covered for each healthcare service and allow you to find your service provider

Track your expenses

Worried how much you’re spending on healthcare? We help you track your expenses including your insurance dues
